
PlotWeaver voices are production assets — multi-speaker, tone-aware, and optimised for narration, dialogue, and conversational interaction. They are not consumer voice clones. Voices are tied to specific language models; not all voices are available for every language.

| VOICE ID | CHARACTER | BEST FOR |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `female_standard` | Clear, neutral female voice | Narration, IVR, education |
| `male_standard` | Clear, neutral male voice | Narration, general synthesis |
| `female_support` | Warm, patient female voice | Customer support agents, tutors |
| `male_dialogue` | Expressive male voice | Dubbing, dramatic content |
